POLYISO: INCREASE THE INSULATION IN OUR WINDOWS

Selection Process

After considering the problem and current State of the Art, we decided to create a cover for windows that more effectively solved the problem of heat transference prevention. While the most efficient way to solve the problem of window heat loss would be to completely remove windows and have thick insulating walls, few customers would agree to a windowless home. We believed that the next best solution would be to construct a movable shade that increased R-value and still met our users’ demands.

Before selecting the most optimal shade construction, we used the matrix below to determine which materials we should use to best increase R-value.
