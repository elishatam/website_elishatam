Problem Definition

1/3 of building heat is lost through windows. We seek to improve window insulation to decrease monetary, energy, and environmental costs in a convenient, inexpensive, and environmentally-friendly way.


Many products have been patented, yet these solutions are incomplete in solving the problem.
Such a task might involve redesigning window structure.
