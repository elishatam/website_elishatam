GEMS OF WISDOM
  • Make a foamcore mockup. It helped us visualize our FLICKER and make appropriate changes.
  • Once we labeled our power cables, we felt more secure about turning on power.
  • Molex wire connectors and superglue them onto your board.
  • Everything appears to work perfectly separately, but when you integrate everything together, bugs arise. Integrate early!
  • Have an up-to-date layout of your C32 pin assignments. It helped us get software and circuits working at the same time.
  • The TA's are really helpful.
