Bill Of Materials 		Our FLICKER was constructed out of 0.5inch thick masonite. The clear floor beneath the computer is made out of acrylic. All component pieces were cut using a LaserCAMM. User directions on the front face of the FLICKER were etched also using the LaserCAMM. We glued the pieces together with Elmer's Wood Glue and hot glue.
