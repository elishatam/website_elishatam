GEMS OF WISDOM
  • The employees at the hobby shops are good resources
  • Daily checklists helped keep us on track.
  • All of our electronics were housed in lid-topped plastic containers. The tight-sealing lids prevented water from entering the containers. However, when we needed to run wire between containers, we first used a clear tube to house the running wires. Then we water-proofed these tubes and any potential leaks with marine grade silicone.
  • Label everything, especially since you will be taking your boat apart all the time.
  • We wished we could have built the boat earlier to run tests earlier.
  • Two team members usually worked on an aspect of the project and that helped with debugging.
