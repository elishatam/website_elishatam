STOVETOP COMMUNICATION DEVICE

Proposed Solution

With this problem in mind, we propose to create a device that will allow for communication between the user and an electric stove via cell phone. The device will have a built-in timer which will signal a computer to dial the cell phone and play a message to the user, indicating whether the stove is on or off. The user can then press 1 to turn the stove off by shutting off the electricity to the appliance or 2 to turn the timer off and leave the stove on if they want to continue cooking their meal (i.e. stew which requires more time.)

The user will also be able to call in the stove at any time to check on the status of the stove and turn it off if necessary.

The device will be an add-on and will be connected between the stove plug and the electric outlet. As a simple add-on that shuts off the electricity to the appliance, our device can potentially be applied to any electric-powered appliance. Users can then check the status of any appliance such as their air-conditioner, iron, or coffeemaker with their cell phone. This is exciting because it has huge market potential.
