ELISHA TAM
Dartmouth '07, BA, Engineering Sciences and Studio Art
Stanford '11, MS, Mechanical Engineering

Hello there! I was born and raised in the Bay Area of California. I attended Dartmouth College in New Hampshire where I made many friends from around the US. Dartmouth opened the door for me to participate in a number of opportunities. I studied abroad in Beijing, traveled to New Orleans for a Jazz Festival with Dartmouth's Gospel Choir, and worked with the Dartmouth community through the Dartmouth Asian Organization and as a Freshmen Orientation Leader.

At Dartmouth, I majored in Engineering Sciences and Studio Art. I continued with my engineering education and obtained my Master's in Mechanical Engineering at Stanford University. My concentration is in Mechatronics where I have learned to create cool electromechanical products.

The people I've met through these activities have inspired me and made these experiences so memorable. I'm grateful for the friends I've made and I look forward to whatever may come next.
