PIRATES OF THE GREAT HALL

Project Summary

The objective of the term project "Pirates of the Great Hall" was to design and fabricate a car that would acquire as many points as possible by picking up balls and pucks, crossing a moat, and placing them into the treasure chest. The moat was 6" wide and the final fabricated car had to fit into the 18"x18" area.
